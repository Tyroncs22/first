chicken with mustard sauce

  • 1/2 lb skinless and bonelesss chicken breast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 egg whutes lightly beaten
  • 2 tsps cornstartch mixed with 1 tb water
  • about 1 1/4 cups of vegetable oil
  • 2 tbs english dry mustard
  • 1tb light soy sauce
  • 1 tb rice vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp seasame oil
  • shredded curly lettuce leaves for serving
  • finely shredded scallions for garnish

ingrediants

  1. thinly shred the chicken and mix first with the salt, then with the egg whites,and finally with the cornstartch paste it is important to mix in this order so that the flavor of the salt will permeate the chicken and the cornstartch coating will be smooth.
  2. heat the oil in a greased and preheated wok until moderatly hot (350). add the chicken and stir with a carving fork. As soon as the chicken turns pale white, quickly scoop it out with strainer and drain.
  3. mix the mustard with a little cold water to for a paste cover and et stand at room temprature for 30 minutes. mix with the soy sauce, vinegar and seasme oil. pile the chicken on a bed of lettuce and pour the sauce over it. garnish and serve cold.
